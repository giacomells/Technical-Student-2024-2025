After further studies we arrived to the conclusion that a tune close to 23 might be the best option to have a good separation at TPST location and a high dispersion at the TECA position.

Indeed a tune scan with a smaller step has been done:
the Q23inspection.ipynb is a tune scan for single turn close to a tune of 23. The step is reduced to 0.01. In this case the separation it has been calculated by the difference of CH particles and non-CH particles that have been tracked for half a turn.

Consequently it is possible to check the separation of a chosen tune with
the file SeparationInspection.ipynb. It can be used to directly check the separation of channeled and non-channeled particles at TPST location after a certain number of turns.
